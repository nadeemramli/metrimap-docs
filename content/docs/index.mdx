Canvasm (the product is also called **Metrimap**) is a platform for building
**metric maps**: visual models of how your business goals connect to the metrics,
data, and work that move them. These docs explain the model, teach the workflow,
and give you the technical reference to connect data and agents.

## What you can do with Canvasm

- **Map your metrics.** Lay out outcomes, the metrics that measure them, and the
  drivers underneath — on an infinite canvas.
- **Connect your data.** Bring numbers in through native connectors or push them
  from your own pipeline, so the map reflects reality.
- **Break down strategy.** Turn objectives into hypotheses and actions, and tie
  each one to the metrics it's expected to move.
- **Share the right view.** Give each team or audience a dashboard scoped to what
  they should see.

Open the app at [use.canvasm.app](https://use.canvasm.app) when you're ready to build.
